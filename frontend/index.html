About Ikimina

Preserving tradition through technology

Ikimina is inspired by the traditional Rwandan community savings system where groups of friends, family, or colleagues pool their money together to help each member achieve financial goals.

Our platform digitizes this time-honored practice, making it more accessible, transparent, and efficient while preserving the community spirit that makes it special.

We believe in financial inclusion and empowerment through community support.
